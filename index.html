Agency

Shark Communications is a creative and digital marketing agency founded by award-winning creative Director, Peter Jacobs. From waterfront, dog-filled offices in Burlington, Vermont, Shark serves clients in a variety of industries with a contemporary mix of branding, marketing, and digital advertising solutions. The agency's creative work receives widespread recognition and provides the core of client marketing programs that integrate multiple media, web development, Search Engine Marketing (SEM) and (SEO), QR code and mobile marketing, social media, and Digital Public Relations.
